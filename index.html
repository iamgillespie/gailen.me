Speedrunner Is Available on Bandcamp

August 29, 2025

Hello friends. I've released a new EP titled Speedrunner. It's kind of a concept idea.

All songs were written in less than a day and were timed. The time it took to complete each song is in the title name of each track.

All song-writing, recording, performance, mixing and mastering we done within those time lines. I had fun throwing these songs together and I feel like it captures a certain raw feel. Hope you enjoy it!
